## üé® Design Philosophy

All content follows GDD principles:

### Jokers
- **25% stackable** (10/40) with qualitative tier upgrades
- **Tier 5 = Ascension** level power (build-defining)
- **Diverse archetypes**: Economy, Rule Bending, Category Amplification, Chaos

### Planets
- **Unlimited stacking**, no downside
- **Pure bonuses** to encourage collection
- **Diverse targets**: Categories, card types, situational bonuses

### Warps
- **Max 3 active** to prevent over-stacking
- **Explicit tradeoffs**: Power comes with blind scaling, resource costs, or restrictions
- **Irreversible** once activated

### Imprints
- **Max 2 per card** for meaningful decisions
- **Four categories**: Economic, Triggering, Conditional, Transformative
- **Persist through shuffles** but destroyed on card removal

### Sculptors
- **Rare/Mythic only** to maintain specialness
- **Heavy costs** (high gold requirements)
- **Permanent effects** on deck composition

### Bosses
- **Counter strategies**: Each boss targets a specific build type
- **Progression**: Easier bosses in Act 1, harder in Act 3
- **Fair but challenging**: Always counterable
